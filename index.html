Harun SERCE

Hello, I'm Harun. My educational background is rooted in Mathematics, where I honed my skills in analytical thinking and problem-solving. However, my career has taken me on an exciting journey beyond the world of equations. After completing my degree, I ventured into the aviation industry, where I found a role as an Air Traffic Controller. This transition felt like a natural fit, combining my technical aptitude with an inherent passion for the field. I've dedicated the past 8 years to mastering the art of air traffic control, with the most recent 5 years spent at Istanbul Sabiha Gökçen International Airport, one of Europe's busiest hubs. This experience has provided me with a unique perspective on managing complex and dynamic environments while ensuring the safety of passengers and aircraft. Beyond aviation, I've developed a keen interest in Data Science, embarking on a journey of continuous learning and skill development in this fascinating field.

I eagerly look forward to connecting with fellow enthusiasts and exploring new opportunities to contribute to the ever-evolving worlds of technology and aviation.
